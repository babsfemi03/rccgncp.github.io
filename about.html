WHO WE ARE AND WHAT IS OUR MISSION?

We Are The Redeemed Christian Church Of God.

The missions for the Redeemed Christian Church Of God Worldwide are;

To make heaven. To take as many people with us. To have a member of RCCG in every family of all nations. To accomplish No. 1 above, holiness will be our lifestyle. To accomplish No. 2 and 3 above, we will plant churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing countries and within fifteen minutes driving distance in every city and town of developed countries. We will pursue these objectives until every Nation in the world is reached for

RCCGNCP

HISTORY

RCCG, New Covenant Pavilion, the baby- Parish of Area 15 of Lagos Province 64 under Region 11. The Parish was Pioneered by 9 adults ably led by Femi Afolabi as the first Parish Pastor of this church beginning from June 2019 when the inaugural service was conducted by Pastor Bamidele Ikotun, APICP (CSR) LP 64. The astonishing and outstanding growth of the parish within a year of its launch is believed to be a function of God's grace working in the Area and mission at large. RCCG, New Covenant Pavilion is fondly perceived to be the home of Worship, Word and Prayer. It is the throne of the almighty God and marvelous testimonies of the members allude to this fact. God answers prayers in special ways in this parish. We believe that with you in the same ship with us will only give us a good ride and safe landing at the end of the journey. We welcome you into our midst.
